REB Submission Support

Get Your Nagano Submission Right the First Time

Preparing a strong REB submission is critical to avoiding delays and ensuring a smooth study start-up.

The ACT-CTU offers pre-submission review of your Nagano package to help identify gaps, improve clarity, and strengthen the overall quality of your submission before it is reviewed by the Research Ethics Board (REB).

What We Offer

Our team provides structured feedback on your Nagano submission package, including:

  • Completeness and internal consistency
  • Clarity of study objectives and design
  • Alignment between protocol, consent, and supporting documents
  • Identification of common REB concerns and potential delays
  • Overall readiness for submission

This review is designed to help you anticipate reviewer questions and reduce back-and-forth during ethics review.

Why This Matters

A well-prepared Nagano submission can:

  • Reduce REB review timelines
  • Minimize requests for revisions
  • Improve clarity for reviewers
  • Accelerate study start-up

Early feedback can make a meaningful difference in both efficiency and success.

Request a Pre-Submission Review

Investigators are invited to submit their Nagano package for review through the RI-MUHC Research Facilitator.

What to Include

To facilitate review, please include:

  • Your completed Nagano submission package
  • Any supporting documents (protocol, consent forms, etc.)
  • Your target submission timeline
